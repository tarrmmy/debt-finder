What Do We Offer You?

Debt Finder App is an online platform that allows school in certain locality to list directory of people owing them academic finances and to keep them from going to other schools.

Our Core Values

Our company values are built upon the satisfaction of our users, we have patterned with over hundreds of users, who have built trust with us over the years. Our principles ensures the free flow of effective managerial skills, Good users experience, and making sure production and usability are implemented.

OUR VISION

To build a community where academic debt can be tracked by the school authorities, reducing the fear of people running-off with the school money.

OUR ACHIEVEMENT

My_Debtorx App has assist over a thousand schools in tracking down their debtors, keeping records of all required data.
